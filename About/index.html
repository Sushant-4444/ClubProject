About Us

The Central Secretarial Club (CSC) was established in 1919 to foster a sense of community and support among secretarial professionals. We provide resources, networking opportunities, and educational programs to help our members thrive in their careers.

Our History

Since its inception in 1919, the Central Secretarial Club has been dedicated to supporting secretarial professionals. Over the decades, we have grown into a vibrant community that offers a wide range of resources and opportunities for professional development.

Our club has a rich history of organizing events, workshops, and seminars that cater to the evolving needs of our members. We take pride in our legacy and continue to build on it by adapting to the changing landscape of the secretarial profession.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower secretarial professionals by providing them with the tools, resources, and support they need to excel in their careers. We are committed to fostering a sense of community and promoting continuous learning and professional development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading organization for secretarial professionals, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and community building. We strive to create an inclusive environment where members can thrive and achieve their full potential.
